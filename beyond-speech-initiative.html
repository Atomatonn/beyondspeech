    Beyond Speech Initiative

    Beyond Speech Initiative, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (T.I.N. 99-1280472) dedicated to empowering Nonspeaking individuals through access to alternative communication, public education, and building supportive communities for Nonspeaking, Minimally Speaking, and Unreliably Speaking individuals and their families.

    Inner Voices Unleashed. Communities Connected.

    Our mission is to empower Nonspeaking, Minimally Speaking, and Unreliably Speaking individuals with tools and support for effective communication and growth. We foster community connections through events and partnerships, providing spaces for shared experiences and resources.

    We believe that communication is so much more than spoken words. It can take many forms - and every individual deserves the chance to be heard in the way that works best for them.

    We honor the strengths and abilities of each person we serve and hold firmly to the belief in their competence, potential, and value.

    By providing access to tools like letterboards and using teaching methods that go beyond traditional therapy, we help nonspeakers find their voices and share their talents.

    Our goal is simple: to create a community where every form of communication is recognized, respected, and celebrated. Together, we can build a world that is more inclusive, equitable, and compassionate for all.

    S2C Scholarships

    Beyond Speech Initiative S2C Scholarship Program

    We’re excited to introduce the Beyond Speech Initiative S2C Scholarship Program, designed to make Spelling to Communicate (S2C) more accessible to those who need it most.


    Scholarship Application Periods

    • October–March
    • April–September

    Families can email bsi@beyondspeechtherapycenter.com for deadline dates or follow Beyond Speech Initiative on Facebook or Instagram for application updates.


    About the Scholarship

    Our scholarship program is available for individuals and families who require financial assistance to participate in S2C services. Funds are awarded bi-annually. The next application deadline is September 22nd for coverage October-March 2026. No late submissions will be accepted.


    Eligibility Requirements

    • The individual you are applying for must be 4 years of age or older.
    • Only parents or legal guardians may apply on behalf of their child/adult.
    • You and your S2C Practitioner must reside in Ohio.
    • Your S2C Practitioner must either be a Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP), an Occupational Therapist (OT), or be supervised by an SLP or OT.
    • A communication partner must be available several days per week and committed to helping the individual learn to effectively use spelling as their primary form of communication.


    Application Process

    To apply for the S2C Scholarship, simply email us at bsi@beyondspeechtherapycenter.com to request the online application form. We’re happy to answer questions about the application/process. The best way to contact us with questions is through the email above.

    Ready to apply? Contact us at bsi@beyondspeechtherapycenter.com today to request an application!
